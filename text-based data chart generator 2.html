DataViz Pro Tutorial

Getting Started

DataViz Pro is a professional data visualization tool designed to help you transform raw data into meaningful insights.

Key Features

  • Multiple Chart Types: Choose from 9 different chart types to best represent your data
  • AI-Powered Insights: Get automatic analysis and predictions from your data
  • Advanced Customization: Fine-tune every aspect of your visualization
  • Export Options: Export charts as SVG, PNG, or PDF
  • Predictive Analytics: See trendlines and future projections

How to Use

  1. Paste your CSV/TSV data into the input area or upload a file
  2. Select your chart type and customization options
  3. Click "Generate Chart" to visualize your data
  4. Use "AI Insights" for automatic data analysis
  5. Export or share your visualization
